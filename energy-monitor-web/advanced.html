Ratio of active power (W) to apparent power (VA). Closer to 1.0 = more efficient. Most home appliances: 0.7–0.95.
〜
Grid Frequency
— Hz
Standard: 50 Hz (Indonesia PLN)
AC grid frequency from PLN. Normal range: 49.5–50.5 Hz. Deviation may indicate grid instability or heavy load conditions.
◈
Apparent Power
— VA
Total power drawn from the grid (V × I). Includes both active (useful) and reactive (wasted) power. Always ≥ active power (W).
∿
Reactive Power
— VAR
Calculated: √(VA² − W²). Power that oscillates between source and load without doing useful work. Caused by inductive/capacitive loads.
